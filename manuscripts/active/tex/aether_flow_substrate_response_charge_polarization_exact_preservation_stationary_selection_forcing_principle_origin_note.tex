\begin{proof}
Item (1) is the forcing-principle construction itself. By assumption, the zero shell of \(\ForSection{\sigma}\) is a closed embedded \(C^2\) submanifold, invariant under the corresponding forcing time reversal and containing the image of the corresponding forcing unit section. The norm-square functional \eqref{eq:forcing_functional} has this shell as its exact zero-energy locus, and the positive normal bound \eqref{eq:forcing_gap} is precisely the current-scope statement that the recorded stationary-selection bundle is not arbitrary but is the stable stationary zero set of the deeper forcing principle.

Item (2) follows by direct restriction along the shell inclusion \(\SelInc{\sigma}\). The restriction of a positive-definite metric to an embedded submanifold is positive definite, and compactness of the visible selection fibers follows because they are closed subsets of compact visible forcing fibers.

Item (3) follows from the restriction formula \eqref{eq:selection_section_from_forcing} together with the shell-restricted norm-square formula \eqref{eq:selection_functional_from_forcing}. Thus neither the recorded stationary-selection sections nor the recorded stationary-selection functionals need to be separately postulated once the forcing layer is fixed.

Items (4) and (5) are immediate from the defining restriction formulas. The target spaces and target metrics are already fixed to be the same recorded ones, and the recorded descendant gap bounds are assumed to survive unchanged on the appropriate descendant shells. Thus no visible slot of the recorded stationary-selection lifted-map package is altered.

Item (6) follows from functoriality of pullback and exterior differentiation:
\[
\SelTwo{\sigma}
=
(\SelInc{\sigma})^*d\ForLiouville{\sigma}
=
d(\SelInc{\sigma})^*\ForLiouville{\sigma}.
\]
Hence the recorded selection Liouville one-forms and closed two-forms are exactly the shell restrictions of the deeper forcing cotangent-model Liouville data.

Item (7) follows because pullback of the skew-Hermitian connection one-form along the shell inclusion gives the recorded selection connection one-form, while parallel transport along a curve in the shell is exactly parallel transport of its included image in the forcing bundle. Identity on constant curves, multiplicativity under concatenation, and inversion under time reversal are inherited without change.

Item (8) is then immediate. Every visible geometric ingredient of the recorded stationary-selection layer is recovered verbatim, but it is no longer primitive.
\end{proof}
