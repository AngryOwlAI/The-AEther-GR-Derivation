At current exact-preservation scope, the pre-ambient substrate bundles, their ambient-generating shell sections, their lifted shell maps and lifted exact-constraint maps with the same target metrics, their pre-ambient cotangent-model Liouville one-forms and closed two-form geometry, their connection one-forms and induced parallel transports, their pre-ambient-basic shadow / rate / density / flux / boundary data, and their fixed-endpoint pre-ambient rate-action geometry are therefore no longer primitive either.
