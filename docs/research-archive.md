This page separates the broader derivational research line from the flagship exact-closure package.

The material collected here now serves two roles: it preserves the earlier bridge/archive record, and it hosts the active continuation of the GR-derivation program. It is still not the benchmark public theory claim by itself, so it should be read only after the exact-closure package has fixed the target that the derivation is supposed to recover.

## Reading Rule

Read the [Theory Package](theory-package.md) first. This derivational material is downstream of the benchmark package, even though it is now the active research workstream.

## What The Archive Contains

- The derivational bridge sequence beginning with [`aether_flow_substrate_kinematics.tex`](../manuscripts/active/tex/aether_flow_substrate_kinematics.tex) and its later continuation through the active manuscript tree under `manuscripts/active/`.
- Technical screening, redesign, and closure notes that remain answerable to the exact-closure benchmark fixed by the flagship package.
- Older tested side branches archived in [`manuscripts/retired/`](../manuscripts/retired).

## How To Read The Archive Honestly

- Treat this page as the active derivational research line plus the surrounding transparency record.
- Do not treat the existence of many bridge manuscripts as evidence that a first-principles derivation of GR has already been completed.
- Keep the claim boundary fixed: the benchmark statement is exact relativistic adoption and exact closure, while deeper substrate derivation remains active open work.
